Hello! I’m Narain. I tinker with machine learning models for a living, diving into the nuts and bolts of deep learning, GPU acceleration, and cloud infrastructures. When I’m not busy breaking stuff, I might be pushing the boundaries of what should (or shouldn’t) be done in C++.

When I’m looking to unwind, I enjoy exploring culinary arts and capturing life’s moments through my lens—usually involving scenes from nature or candid shots of daily life.
